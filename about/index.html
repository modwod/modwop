About

Modular

Modular is the sum of many calculated artistic decisions.

The musical art that is derived from a modular system starts with an idea for a module that aims to make, manipulate or modulate sound in a unique manner.

That idea is first carefully planned and designed onto a circuit board.

The front panel of the module is the interface to the unique circuits, sometimes with simple labels, or a work of art by itself.

A modular system is a work of art, as each module has to be chosen, placed and planned on how it will be patched together in the overall musical instrument.

And lastly, the modular music performace is the artistic sum of it all.

The musical styles and compositions are endless, from more traditonal, danceable 4-on-the-floor Electronic Dance Music, to more abstract and ambient.

Eurorack

Eurorack is a global modular standard created by Dieter Doepfer. There are hundreds of eurorack module designers & manufactures all over the world, from DIY hobbiest, to single person shops, to dedicated Eurorack modular companies.

Modular and Eurorack are often, and can be, used interchangeably, but there are many other modular standards and form factors.
